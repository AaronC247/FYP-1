About

The basis of this project is to create a spatial profiling system for a residential property search within the Republic of Ireland. The data used to create the spatial profiles consist mostly of information from the Central Statistics Office Census 2016, along with a small number of other datasets. The locations being spatially profiled are Census small areas; these areas are generally small and populated by between 80 and 120 dwellings. Users are able to specify their ideal living area either by a set of location-based questions or by comparing locations to a selected area. The result of this process is a map that displays a colour-coded heat map layer detailing the suitability of areas on the map. This should allow individuals to relocate to the best location that meets their requirements.
